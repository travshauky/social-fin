# AAOIFI

**Accounting and Auditing Organization for Islamic Financial Institutions; international standard-setting body for Islamic finance.**

## Summary

AAOIFI is an independent, nonprofit organization that develops accounting, auditing, governance, ethics, and Sharia standards for Islamic financial institutions globally. Established to ensure consistency, transparency, and Sharia compliance across the Islamic finance industry, AAOIFI issues Sharia Standards (SS), Accounting and Auditing Standards (AAOIFI Standards), and Guidelines for Governance. Its work is recognized by regulators, central banks, and Islamic financial institutions worldwide.

## Key Standards

- **Sharia Standards (SS)** — Guidance on Islamic financial contracts and structures
  - SS-46: Al-Wakalah Bi Al-Istithmar (Investment Agency)
  - SS-1 through SS-45: Contracts including Murabahah, Musharaka, Ijarah, etc.
- **Accounting Standards** — Financial reporting standards for Islamic institutions
- **Auditing Standards** — Audit practices specific to Islamic finance
- **Governance Guidelines** — Board and management practices for Islamic banks

## Mission

To develop international standards that:
- Ensure Sharia compliance in Islamic financial products
- Promote transparency and accountability
- Enable cross-border consistency in Islamic finance
- Support the growth and stability of the Islamic finance industry
