# AI Forecasting

**The practice of making concrete, scoreable predictions about AI capability trajectories, deployment, and societal impact — as opposed to vague gestures at "AGI soon."**

## Summary
AI forecasting tries to convert hand-wavy AI futurism into specific claims you can later check. Methods include trend extrapolation (compute, parameter count, benchmark performance), tabletop exercises / wargames, expert elicitation, and structured scenario writing. The [[ai-2027-scenario]] is a leading example of the scenario-writing approach.

## Key points
- Concreteness is the point — vague predictions can't be falsified.
- Trend extrapolation works well for compute and dollars, less well for capabilities.
- Tabletop exercises surface considerations that single-author writing misses (the AI 2027 team ran ~25).
- Track records matter — see [[daniel-kokotajlo]]'s 2021 predictions as a calibration anchor.
