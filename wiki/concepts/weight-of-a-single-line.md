# The Weight of a Single Line

**One-line definition.**

## Summary
A single poetic line carries compressed meaning and works like a brushstroke or film edit.

## Key points
- Poetry compresses meaning into a line, creating resonance through placement and silence. ([[2026-06-29--the-weight-of-a-single-line]] )
- The line is both linguistic and spatial, and the break creates pause. ([[2026-06-29--the-weight-of-a-single-line]] )
- Compression is a useful skill across art forms and in life. ([[2026-06-29--the-weight-of-a-single-line]] )
