# Second Brain

**An external, persistent knowledge system that captures, organizes, and compounds what you learn — so memory and synthesis don't bottleneck on biological recall.**

## Summary
Umbrella term for personal knowledge management systems. The [[llm-wiki-pattern]] is a specific, LLM-native take on the idea: instead of the human doing the filing, the LLM does it, and the human curates.

## Key points
- Goal: turn one-shot reading into compounding understanding.
- Failure mode of most second brains: maintenance cost grows faster than value, user abandons.
- The [[llm-wiki-pattern]] tries to solve this by offloading bookkeeping to the LLM.
