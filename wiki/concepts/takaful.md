# Takaful

**One-line definition.** Mutual insurance based on Shariah principles of shared risk and voluntary contribution (tabarru').

## Summary
Takaful is a Shariah-compliant mutual protection system where participants contribute to a pooled fund to cover predefined risks. It emphasizes risk-sharing, ethical investment, and avoidance of riba and gharar. Operationally, Takaful distinguishes Family and General lines and follows Shariah governance (Shariah Committee) and reporting standards such as AAOIFI.
