**One-line definition.**

## Summary
Applying painters' concepts of contrast, temperature, saturation, and restraint to poetic craft.

## Key points
- Painters use warm/cool contrast the way poets use sound and diction. ([[2026-06-29--color-theory-for-poets]] )
- Complementary visual pairs map to juxtaposition in poetry. ([[2026-06-29--color-theory-for-poets]] )
