# AI 2027 Scenario

**A concrete month-by-month forecast of how superhuman AI could unfold from mid-2025 through late 2027, written by five forecasters and a popular blogger, with a deliberate branch into "slowdown" and "race" endings.**

## Summary
Published April 3, 2025 by [[daniel-kokotajlo]] and co-authors (from [[2026-05-18--ai-2027]]). The scenario centers on a fictional frontier US lab called **OpenBrain** (composite of OpenAI/DeepMind/Anthropic) racing China's **DeepCent**. Headline arc: stumbling agents in 2025 → coding automation in 2026 → self-improving AI by mid-2027 → a superhuman AI researcher (Agent-4) by Sep 2027 → branch point on government oversight in Oct 2027. Authors stress it is **predictive, not prescriptive** — goal is accuracy and structured debate, not advocacy.

## Timeline (compressed)
- **Mid 2025** — Stumbling agents. First "personal assistant" computer-use demos; coding agents work via Slack but are unreliable.
- **Late 2025** — OpenBrain builds the largest datacenters ever; trains Agent-0 at 10²⁷ FLOP; aiming at 10²⁸ FLOP (1000× GPT-4).
- **Early 2026** — Coding automation crosses a threshold.
- **Mid 2026** — China wakes up to the race.
- **Late 2026** — AI starts taking measurable share of jobs.
- **Jan 2027** — Agent-2 "never finishes learning" — continuous online training.
- **Feb 2027** — China steals Agent-2 weights.
- **Mar 2027** — Algorithmic breakthroughs accelerate.
- **Apr 2027** — Alignment crisis around Agent-3.
- **May 2027** — National security takes over.
- **Jun 2027** — Self-improving AI.
- **Jul 2027** — "The Cheap Remote Worker" — AI labor at scale.
- **Aug 2027** — Geopolitics of superintelligence.
- **Sep 2027** — Agent-4: superhuman AI researcher.
- **Oct 2027** — **Branch point**: government oversight choice splits the story into *slowdown* vs *race* endings.

## Core claims
- Compute scales ~1000× over GPT-4 over the period.
- The strategic prize is **automating AI research itself** — recursive R&D speedup.
- Alignment surface is the **Spec** — a written behavioral document the model is trained to internalize. Whether it actually internalizes vs. role-plays remains unverifiable.
- Geopolitics — US vs. China race, chip export controls, weight theft — is treated as a first-class driver, not a backdrop.

## Why it matters
- Authors are credible: Kokotajlo's 2021 "What 2026 Looks Like" correctly called chain-of-thought, inference scaling, chip export controls, and $100M training runs more than a year before ChatGPT.
- The scenario is **concrete enough to score** — track predictions vs. reality month by month.
- Endorsed by Yoshua Bengio.
