# The Museum as Writing Prompt

**One-line definition.**

## Summary
Museum visits can be a structured source of writing prompts and sustained attention.

## Key points
- Use a notebook and leave the phone behind to deepen attention. ([[2026-06-29--the-museum-as-writing-prompt]] )
- Practice slow looking, overheard fragments, and wrong captions. ([[2026-06-29--the-museum-as-writing-prompt]] )
- The best poems often arrive after the visit, once the images settle. ([[2026-06-29--the-museum-as-writing-prompt]] )
