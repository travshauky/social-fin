Perspective of Thinking in Islam 
★ The Islamic Perspective on Intellect / thinking: Islam  
places a profound emphasis on the importance of  
thinking and contemplation before making decisions. 
★ A Divine Gift: Humans are endowed with intellect and  
reason as a fundamental part of their creation. 
★ Quranic Foundation: The Holy Quran contains  
numerous verses that command and encourage the use  
of the mind 
 COR 0702 - Week 2
Concepts of Critical Thinking (Tafakkur) 
★ Definition: In an Islamic context, "Critical Thinking" is often  
associated with the concept of Tafakkur (contemplation). 
★ Methodology: It involves using one's intellect to analyze  
information and learn through observation and reflection. 
★ Linguistic Roots: Scholars like Malik Badri distinguish  
between Tafkir (general thinking) and Tafakkur (deep,  
meditative reflection). Al-Jawhari describes Tafakkur as a  
state of deep, quiet focus. 
 COR 0702 - Week 2
The Five Dimensions of Islamic Thinking 
To develop a comprehensive understanding, Islam  
encourages five distinct styles of thought: 
★ Tazakkur: Reflection and learning from  
lessons/reminders. 
★ Tafakkur: Deep contemplation of creation and the  
Creator. 
★ Ta’abbur: to observe carefully 
★ Ta’akul: Using reason and logic to understand the  
reality of things. 
★ Tadabbur: Deliberation and pondering over the  
consequences and deeper meanings (often applied  
to the Quran) 
 COR 0702 - Week 2
Thafakkur 


Al-Fikr (Thought) 
Considered an abstract act of reflection.  Linguists often view it as the synonym of  Tafakkur, yet it represents the foundational  mental impulse or concept. 
Tafakkur (Thinking) 
A deliberate and systematic reflective  discourse. It is a process that bridges  perception with conception—connecting the  creation to the Creator.

 COR 0702 - Week 2 
The Three Stages of Reflection/Tafakkur 
Professor Malik Badri's Model 
Information Gathering: Data derived from sense  perception, imagination, or abstraction. 
Aesthetic Appreciation: Acknowledging traits and  perfection, leading to wonderment. 
Cognitive Transcendence: Pondering the attributes of  the Creator to strengthen faith.
 COR 0702 - Week 2 
Quranic Linguistic Patterns 
Verbal Emphasis: The concept is used 18 times as a verb, emphasizing the process of thinking rather than  an abstract noun. 
Temporal Continuity: 17 out of 18 instances use the present tense, indicating that reflection must be an  ongoing, continuous act. 
Collective Intelligence: Present tense forms are plural, highlighting the importance of "Shuratic" or collective  communal thinking. 
Dynamic Exegesis: Most commentators interpret Tafakkur as contemplation—a path toward growth, not a  static terminal outcome.
 COR 0702 - Week 2 
The Spiritual Significance of Contemplation 
★ A Bridge to Faith: Tafakkur serves as a bridge that  
connects the soul to the Creator through the wonders  
of the universe and the prospect of the Hereafter. 
★ Appreciation of Creation: It involves paying full  
attention to the beauty and complexity of the world,  
leading to a sense of awe and gratitude. 
★ Strengthening Belief: Reflecting on the attributes of  
Allah through His creation strengthens one's Iman 
(faith). 
★ As an Act of Worship: In Islam, thinking with a sincere  
intention and a good purpose is considered a form of  
worship. 
 COR 0702 - Week 2
Standards for Shaping Human Thought 
★ The World: Thinking about the nature of  
worldly life. 
★ Social Life: Reflecting on how to shape and  
improve human social interactions. 
★ Self-Accountability: Engaging in  
"Self-Accounting" (Muhasabah) to reflect on  
one's own actions and character. 
★ Future Planning: Using available information  
to contemplate and prepare for future events 
 COR 0702 - Week 2
Conclusion & Practical Benefits 
★ Dynamic Process: Quranic terminology  
suggests that Tafakkur is an ongoing process,  
not just a one-time result. 
★ Holistic Growth: By following these  
standards, individuals develop a balanced  
perspective that incorporates logic, social  
responsibility, and spiritual growth.
